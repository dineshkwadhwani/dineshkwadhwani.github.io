About Me

Over three decades I have built and led global engineering organisations delivering large enterprise platforms across multiple industries. As a senior technology leader I have managed global R&D teams of more than 500 professionals, delivered product portfolios exceeding $500M, and scaled engineering organisations across multiple geographies. Beyond technology execution, my passion has been leadership development — mentoring professionals, enabling leaders to grow into strategic roles, and building high-performance cultures. Today I combine my executive experience with structured coaching frameworks to help professionals elevate their leadership presence, sharpen decision-making, and accelerate career impact.
